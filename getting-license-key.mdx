---
title: 'Getting a License Key'
description: 'How to generate and activate your Lumi Mint Bot (Beta) license'
---

Lumi Mint Bot (Beta) requires a license key to run. Keys are issued through our Discord server.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the Discord server">
    Join [discord.gg/pulseanchor](https://discord.gg/pulseanchor) if you haven't already.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get the Crew or Partner role">
    Generating a key requires the **Crew** or **Partner** role in the server.

    - **Crew** represents consistent, active participation and contribution in the community. To keep the role meaningful, inactive Crew members are periodically removed. If you're removed, you can reapply through the application process.
    - **Partner** is reserved for members from our partner projects.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the slash command">
    In the `🤖│bot-commands` channel, type:

    ```
    /generate-license-key
    ```

    The bot will reply privately (only you can see it) with your key. It looks like this:

    ```
    A1B2-C3D4-E5F6-G7H8-I9J0-K1L2-M3N4-O5P6-Q7R8-S9T0
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy it into your config file">
    Paste the key into the `license_key` field of your settings file. See [Configuring the Bot](/configuration#authentication).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important things to know

<Warning title="One key = one computer">
  The first time your key is checked, it locks to that computer. If you try to use the same key on a second computer, it will be rejected. If you need to move to a new PC, re-run `/generate-license-key` in Discord. This generates the same key again but resets which machine it's locked to.
</Warning>

<Note title="Keys expire after 30 days">
  Running `/generate-license-key` again before or after expiry refreshes it for another 30 days and unlocks it from whatever machine it was previously tied to.
</Note>

If your key is rejected and you don't know why, the bot will print the exact reason it received from the license server. See [Troubleshooting](/troubleshooting#license-errors).
