**Wallet Tools** (from the [main menu](/07-main-menu)) is a separate menu of utilities. Nothing here mints an NFT, it's for managing the wallets you already have.

```
Wallet Tools:
  WL Checker
  Check Token Balance
  Disperse Tokens
  Consolidate Tokens
  Disperse NFT
  Consolidate NFT
  ⌂  Main Menu
```

## WL Checker

Checks which of your wallets are allowlisted for a drop, without minting anything.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a platform">
    OpenSea, Blever, or Transient.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the slug">
    Leave blank to use the slug already in your config.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the summary">
    ```
    === Eligibility Summary ===
    Stage: Allowlist
      main    - 0x1a2b...9f8e - 2 remaining
      backup  - 0x3c4d...7a6b - not eligible
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Check Token Balance

Checks how much of a token your wallets hold, on any configured chain.

1. Pick a chain (from your configured `rpcs`).
2. Enter an ERC-20 contract address, or leave blank to check the native token (ETH, etc.) instead.
3. Select which wallets to check.

You get a table of balances per wallet plus a total.

## Disperse Tokens

Sends a set amount of a token from **one** wallet out to **multiple** destination wallets. Useful for funding several mint wallets from one main wallet before a drop.

1. Pick a chain and a token (blank = native token).
2. Pick one **source** wallet.
3. Pick one or more **destination** wallets.
4. Enter the amount to send to each.
5. For native tokens, choose whether to force-send the full amount to every destination, or only "top up" wallets that currently hold less than the target amount (skipping ones that already have enough).
6. Confirm, and watch the per-transfer ✅/❌ results.

## Consolidate Tokens

The reverse of Disperse: sweeps a token from **multiple** wallets into **one** destination. Useful for collecting funds back after a mint.

1. Pick a chain and a token (blank = native token).
2. Pick one or more **source** wallets.
3. Pick one **destination** wallet.
4. Enter an amount per wallet, or leave blank to sweep everything (minus a small reserve left behind to cover that wallet's own gas fee).

## Disperse NFT

Spreads NFTs you own in one wallet out across several of your other wallets.

1. Pick a chain and enter the contract address or OpenSea slug.
2. Pick the source wallet and destination wallet(s).
3. The bot lists the NFTs that wallet owns from that collection.
4. Choose an assignment mode:
   - **Automatic (round-robin)**: pick which NFTs to move, and the bot spreads them evenly across your chosen destinations.
   - **Manual (choose per wallet)**: you assign specific token IDs to specific destination wallets yourself.

## Consolidate NFT

The reverse: gathers NFTs from several of your wallets into one destination wallet.

1. Pick a chain and enter the contract address or slug.
2. Pick one or more source wallets and one destination wallet.
3. The bot shows every NFT your selected wallets own from that collection (grouped by wallet).
4. Check the ones you want to move. They're transferred from whichever wallet currently owns each one.
