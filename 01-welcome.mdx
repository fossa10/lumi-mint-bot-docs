Lumi Mint Bot is a desktop tool that helps you mint NFTs the moment a drop opens, across multiple wallets, without you having to sit refreshing a page and clicking "Mint" by hand.

It runs as a simple menu you navigate with your arrow keys and Enter — no coding required.

## What it can do

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="OpenSea drops" icon="rocket">
    Mint from any OpenSea SeaDrop collection, across multiple wallets and allowlist stages.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Blever drops" icon="rocket">
    Mint from Blever.xyz drops using the same wallet and stage-based workflow.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Transient drops" icon="rocket">
    Mint from Transient.xyz (Transient Labs) drops, including presale allowlist checks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom contracts" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Mint from almost any NFT contract, even ones not listed on a supported platform, by letting the bot discover the mint function for you.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

It also includes a **Wallet Tools** menu for everyday wallet management: checking allowlist eligibility, checking token balances, and moving tokens or NFTs between your own wallets.

## How it works, in one paragraph

You tell the bot which wallets to use and which drop to target by editing one settings file. When you run the bot, you pick a platform, pick which wallets to mint with, pick the sale stage you're eligible for, and then choose a **mode** — for example, "wait for the drop to open and mint automatically" or "just try to mint right now." The bot then handles the technical parts: checking eligibility, building the transaction, paying gas, and submitting it to the blockchain.

## Before you start

You'll need three things ready before the bot is useful to you:

2. One or more **wallets** funded with enough crypto to mint and pay gas — see [Setting Up Your Wallets](/wallet-setup).
3. A few minutes to fill in the **settings file** — see [Configuring the Bot](/configuration).

Once those are done, head to [Installation & First Run](/installation).
